## 3. Empirical Research: The Vanishing Gradient Problem
My most significant technical contribution was the numerical proof of the **Vanishing Gradient** problem.

* **Experiment:** I captured the gradient norms of the first linear layer's weights during the initial backward pass.
* **Findings:** * **Sigmoid:** Resulted in a gradient norm of **0.001954**, showing extreme signal attenuation.
    * **ReLU:** Resulted in a gradient norm of **0.107043**.
* **Conclusion:** I mathematically proved that **ReLU provides a ~54.8x stronger learning signal** than Sigmoid, which is the fundamental reason why our deep model converged significantly faster with ReLU.
